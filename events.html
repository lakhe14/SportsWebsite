How to Register for Events

Participating in our sports events is easy! Follow these simple steps to register:

  1. Check Event Details: Review the event information including date, time, location, and requirements.
  2. Verify Eligibility: Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for the specific event (age, skill level, etc.).
  3. Complete Registration: Fill out the online registration form with your personal and contact information.
  4. Submit Required Documents: Upload any required documents such as student ID, medical certificates, or previous performance records.
  5. Pay Registration Fee: Complete the payment process if there's a registration fee for the event.
  6. Receive Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation email with event details and instructions.
  7. Attend Pre-event Meeting: Some events may require attendance at a pre-event briefing or practice session.

Note: Registration deadlines vary by event, so make sure to register early to secure your spot!
